Origin

What Is the Asten Vale Archives?

The Asten Vale Archives is a mystery experience built around music. Each case is a song — and inside the song, if you listen closely enough, are clues. Evidence. Things that were never supposed to be found.

Every case file contains lyrics, hidden notes, and fragments of something larger. Whether you follow them is up to you.

"The truth is in the details. It always has been."

The Archivist

Who Keeps These Records?

The Archive was assembled by a single archivist — a songwriter who became obsessed with documenting what happened in Asten Vale. Their identity remains ██████████████, and their whereabouts are unknown.

What they left behind was a collection. Cases. Songs. Notes in the margins. A record of things the town would rather forget.

How It Works

The Method

New cases are released one at a time. Each unlocked file includes a song, liner notes, and case materials for those paying attention. Locked cases are coming — when they open depends on what gets uncovered.

Join the Archive to be notified when new cases are declassified. Some files are only available to those on record.
