The Method

How To Solve A Case

Each case file contains a song, a witness, and a trail of evidence. Your job is to listen and find what the archive has buried.

🎧
Listen To The Song

Every case is built around one original song. The music holds the clues — not just the lyrics, but what's between them.

👤
Hear The Testimony

A witness recorded their account. Their words are partial. Their silences matter more than anything they said.

🔍
Find Seven Answers

Location. Object. Witness. Time. Marker. Direction. Sound. All seven are hidden inside the case. None of them are guesses.

🏗
Unlock The Archive

Solve enough cases to unseal the next season. Complete a full season to claim its vault — a reward no one else can decode for you.

What's In The Archive

Every Feature Of The Investigation

Built as a complete investigative experience. Not a quiz. Not a puzzle game. A working archive with real evidence.

🎧
Original Songs

Every case is anchored to an original song. The music is the evidence. Stream on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and Amazon Music — or play direct from the archive.

👤
Witness Testimonies

Each case includes a recorded witness statement. Audio only. Read between the words. The archive does not always tell you who to trust.

🔊
Evidence Recordings

Environmental audio recovered from case sites. 70+ unique recordings spanning animal, weather, structure, mechanical, human, and nature categories.

📷
Location Photography

Every case site has a recovered photograph. Exactly as it was found. Exactly as the archive sealed it.

📜
Investigation Reports

A formal archive report accompanies every solved case, including a recovered novel audio reading — the full written account of the investigation.

📋
Recovered Artefacts

Objects, witness portraits, markers, sound records, direction logs, and time stamps. All seven clue types have a physical artefact in the cabinet.

📚
Archived Journals

Field notes recovered from inside each case file. The investigator's private record. Often the only source that disagrees with the official report.

🔒
Season Progression

50 seasons unlock in sequence. Solve a full season to advance. Cases you haven't earned are classified. Cases in unreleased seasons are sealed.

🏛
Season Vaults

Complete all 12 cases in a season and the vault unlocks. Each vault contains a badge, an exclusive lore entry, and a unique archive coupon code.
